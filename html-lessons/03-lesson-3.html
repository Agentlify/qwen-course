From One Assistant to a Whole Team

So far, you've been talking to ONE Qwen. You ask it something. It does it. You ask something else. It does that. One task at a time. Like having one team member.

What if you had THREE?

Not three separate conversations. Three Qwens, working at the SAME TIME, on different tasks.

While you sit there and drink your coffee.
