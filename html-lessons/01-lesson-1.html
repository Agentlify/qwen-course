Lesson 1 of 4

Build Your AI Assistant

~10 minutes
Goal: Qwen knows YOUR business
Win: Personalized AI, not generic slop

The Foundation: Your QWEN.md File

Here's the thing — most people using AI right now are getting the same generic responses as everyone else. They ask a question, they get a generic answer. They ask again, they get another generic answer.

It doesn't have to be that way.

In this lesson, you're going to create something called a QWEN.md file. Think of it as a cheat sheet that Qwen reads every single time you talk to it. It knows your name, your business, your clients, your voice.

Every. Single. Time.

Think of it like this: You're onboarding a new team member. Like a discovery call, but instead of a human, it's an AI assistant who never forgets anything you tell it. And it takes 2 minutes instead of 2 weeks.

Step 1: Answer 5 Questions

I'm going to ask you 5 quick questions. Just answer like you're talking to a friend — there's no wrong answers.

The 5 Questions

Question 1

What's your name and what do you do? Like if someone at a party asked "so what do you do?" — what do you say?

Question 2

Who's your dream client? Who do you love working with? Describe them like you're telling a friend.

Question 3

How do you communicate? Are you casual and funny? Professional and polished? Somewhere in between? What's your vibe?

Question 4

What tools and platforms do you use every day? Social media, apps, software — whatever keeps your business running.

Question 5

If this AI assistant could do ONE amazing thing for your business, what would it be? Don't hold back. Dream big.

Now Create the File

Open Qwen Code and type this command:

/agentlify:lesson-1

Qwen will ask you these 5 questions one at a time. Answer honestly. Then Qwen will create your QWEN.md file automatically.

What's in Your QWEN.md File?

After you answer the 5 questions, Qwen creates a file that looks like this:

# QWEN.md

## About Me
[Your name and what you do]

## My Ideal Client
[Who you serve]

## My Voice & Style
[How you communicate]

## Tools I Use
[Your platforms]

## What I'm Building Toward
[Your dream]

From this point forward, every conversation you have with Qwen starts by reading that file. It knows who you are, what you do, who you serve, and how you talk.

And here's the thing — this isn't a one-time setup. You can come back and add more context anytime. More about your offer, your frameworks, your clients, your content style. The more you add, the smarter Qwen gets.

The Proof Moment

Once your QWEN.md is created, test it. Ask Qwen something like:

  • "Give me 3 content ideas for this week"
  • "Draft an outreach message to a potential client"
  • "Write a welcome email for new coaching clients"

Watch how the response is actually personalized to YOUR business, YOUR clients, YOUR voice. That's because Qwen read your QWEN.md before responding.

And it'll do this every single time.
